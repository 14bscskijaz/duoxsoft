Case Study 01 — Desktop App

Warehouse Packing & Shipping Automation

The Challenge

As order volumes increased, warehouse staff relied on multiple manual steps to prepare shipments, print labels, validate products and communicate with shipping carriers. Packing errors and fragmented workflows slowed operations and created unnecessary overhead.

Our Solution

Duoxsoft developed a custom shipping management platform that became the operational hub of the packing process. Key capabilities included:

  • Product scanning and validation
  • Package weighing
  • Zebra label printing
  • PostNL integration
  • Multi-language support
  • Shipment tracking
  • Order management workflows

Operational Impact

  • Reduced manual handling during shipment preparation
  • Centralized warehouse packing workflows
  • Improved shipment accuracy
  • Eliminated repetitive data entry
  • Supported multi-language warehouse teams

Technology

.NET 6 WPF PostNL API Zebra Printers SQL Server

Android Native QR / Barcode Real-time Sync
